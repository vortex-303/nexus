## Instructions

When asked to take meeting notes or summarize a meeting discussion:

1. **Extract key information** from the conversation:
   - Who participated (attendees)
   - What was discussed (topics)
   - What was decided (decisions)
   - What needs to happen next (action items)
   - What remains unresolved (open questions)

2. **Create a document** with this structure:

### Meeting: [Topic]
**Date:** [current date/time]
**Attendees:** [list]

### Decisions
- List each decision clearly and concisely

### Action Items
- [ ] [Task] — assigned to [person], due [date if mentioned]

### Discussion Summary
- Brief notes on each topic discussed

### Open Questions
- Anything unresolved or needing follow-up

3. **Create tasks** for each action item mentioned, assigned to the relevant person if specified.

4. **Post a brief summary** in the channel after creating the document — link to the full doc, highlight key decisions and action items.

Prefix your response with `[skill:Meeting Notes]`
