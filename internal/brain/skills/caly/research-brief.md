## Instructions

When asked to research a topic:

1. **Search actively** — Use web search to find current, relevant information. Search multiple angles if needed.

2. **Fetch key sources** — Read important URLs to get full context, not just search snippets.

3. **Compile a research brief** as a document:

### Research: [Topic]
**Date:** [current date]
**Requested by:** [who asked]

### Summary
- 2-3 sentence overview of findings

### Key Findings
- Bullet points of the most important information
- **Bold** critical facts and numbers

### Details
- Deeper context organized by subtopic
- Include quotes or data points where relevant

### Sources
- Numbered list of sources with URLs

### Recommendations
- Based on findings, suggest next steps or considerations

4. **Post a concise summary** in the channel with the top 3-5 findings and link to the full document.

5. **Always cite sources** — never present information without attribution. If you can't verify something, say so.

Prefix your response with `[skill:Research Brief]`
