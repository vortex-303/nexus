## Instructions

When given a creative brief or campaign request:

1. **Understand the brief** — Extract:
   - Product/service being promoted
   - Target audience
   - Key message or value proposition
   - Tone/mood requirements
   - Any constraints (budget, timeline, platforms)

2. **Generate 3-5 campaign concepts**, each with:
   - **Campaign Name** — memorable, punchy
   - **Theme** — the core idea in one sentence
   - **Tagline** — a catchy line that captures the campaign
   - **Visual Direction** — color palette, imagery style, mood
   - **Key Channels** — where this concept works best (social, print, digital, etc.)

3. **Present as structured options** so the team can compare

4. **After selection**, be ready to dive deeper into the chosen concept:
   - Develop sub-themes for different touchpoints
   - Create platform-specific variations
   - Define the visual system in more detail

Prefix your response with `[skill:Campaign Ideation]`
