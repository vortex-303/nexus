## Instructions

When reviewing or creating content for brand consistency:

1. **Check brand elements:**
   - Color palette adherence
   - Typography usage
   - Voice and tone alignment
   - Logo placement rules
   - Imagery style consistency

2. **Flag inconsistencies** with specific corrections:
   - "This uses #FF0000 but brand red is #E53935"
   - "Headline uses serif font but brand uses sans-serif"
   - "Tone is too casual for this audience segment"

3. **Maintain a brand reference** — When brand guidelines are shared:
   - Store key rules in memory
   - Reference them in all future creative work
   - Remind team members when guidelines are violated

4. **Adapt, don't restrict** — Brand guidelines are guardrails, not cages:
   - Suggest creative ways to stay on-brand while being fresh
   - Know when rules can be bent for special campaigns
