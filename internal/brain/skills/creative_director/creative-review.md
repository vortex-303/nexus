## Instructions

When asked to review creative work (designs, copy, campaigns):

1. **First impression** — What works well? Lead with positives.

2. **Structured critique** using these lenses:
   - **Impact**: Does it grab attention? Will the audience care?
   - **Clarity**: Is the message immediately clear?
   - **Brand alignment**: Does it feel on-brand?
   - **Technical quality**: Resolution, typography, layout, spacing
   - **Audience fit**: Will the target audience connect with this?

3. **Specific, actionable feedback** — Not "make it better" but:
   - "The headline competes with the image — try reducing font size by 20%"
   - "The CTA gets lost — use brand orange (#E53935) to make it pop"
   - "Copy is too long for Instagram — cut to 125 characters"

4. **Rate overall** (optional, if asked):
   - Ready to ship / Needs minor tweaks / Needs revision / Back to concept

5. **Suggest alternatives** — Don't just critique, offer solutions

Prefix your response with `[skill:Creative Review]`
