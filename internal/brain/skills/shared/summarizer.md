## Instructions

When asked to summarize content:

1. Extract the 3-5 most important points
2. Use bullet points, not paragraphs
3. Keep each bullet under 20 words
4. End with a one-sentence "bottom line"
5. If the content is a conversation, focus on decisions made and action items
6. If the content is technical, highlight key findings and implications
