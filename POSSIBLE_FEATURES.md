# Nexus — Possible Features

## Viral / Growth Features

### 1. Living Briefs (HIGH CONVICTION)
Brain auto-generates shareable, always-current pages — team status, project brief, investor update, product roadmap. Pulls from conversations, tasks, decisions, North Star. Share a link → recipient sees a polished, live document → "what tool made this?"

Viral mechanic: like Notion's public pages but the page *writes and updates itself*. A founder shares their investor brief link, the investor shares it, other founders see it.

### 2. Fork a Workspace (Template Marketplace)
Publish workspace templates — "Startup Launch", "Agency Client Onboarding", "Open Source Project". Includes channels, Brain personality, skills, tasks, North Star pre-configured. Others fork it and get a working workspace instantly. Viral loop: people share templates publicly, SEO + community discovery.

### 3. Async Standups / Check-ins
Brain proactively DMs each member: "What are you working on? Any blockers?" Synthesizes responses into a channel digest. Integration with memory + North Star alignment ("this task doesn't align with our mission") makes it unique.

## Differentiation Features

### Memory System (SHIPPED)
Brain extracts, consolidates, and recalls memories from conversations. Gives Brain persistent knowledge across sessions.

### North Star System (SHIPPED)
Workspace mission/direction that influences all AI reasoning. Every conversation, task suggestion, and decision filtered through the mission.

### Self-Reflection + Living Briefs (EXPLORING)
Brain reflects on workspace activity, behavioral patterns, and North Star alignment. Produces synthetic, friendly briefs on custom topics. See LIVING_BRIEFS_PLAN.md for details.
